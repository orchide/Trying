COVID-19: Our Response

In an effort to keep our community safe and maintain the current best practices in response to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have decided to close the church office until further notice. Our staff team will continue to be working remotely from our homes, and small teams practicing safe social distancing will be continuing essential work from the church building that cannot be conducted offsite. Our church phone line will still be open, and we will have access to any messages left at the office via the phone (613) 899-5235
